Welcome to XAgri! 🚜🌾

At XAgri, we are dedicated to revolutionizing farming through innovation and sustainability. Our project includes:

  • 🌍 CO2 Detector: Helping to monitor and reduce carbon emissions in farming.
  • 🤖 Ploughing Robot: A robot designed to plough fields efficiently and reduce manual labor.
  • 🌐 Website: A platform to access project data, updates, and more!

Our Missions 🚀

Our mission is to contribute to sustainable development by addressing the following Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs):

  • 🌱 SDG 13 - Climate Action: Reducing the carbon footprint of agriculture.
  • 🔋 SDG 7 - Affordable and Clean Energy: Innovating clean energy solutions for farming machinery.
  • 💼 SDG 8 - Decent Work and Economic Growth: Creating job opportunities in tech and agriculture.
  • 🏙️ SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities: Building resilient farming systems.
  • 🌳 SDG 15 - Life on Land: Promoting biodiversity and sustainable land use.
